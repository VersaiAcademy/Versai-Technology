Our Backup Process

A proven methodology to ensure your data is always protected

1

Assessment & Planning

We analyze your data landscape, compliance requirements, and recovery objectives to design the optimal backup strategy.

2

Implementation

Our engineers deploy the backup solution with minimal disruption to your operations.

3

Initial Backup

We perform a full initial backup, often using seed loading for large datasets.

4

Incremental Backups

Automated incremental backups capture only changed data to minimize bandwidth usage.

5

Verification & Monitoring

Regular integrity checks and 24/7 monitoring ensure backup reliability.

6

Recovery Testing

We conduct periodic recovery drills to validate your ability to restore data when needed.
